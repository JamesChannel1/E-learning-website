Overview

In these tests it is all about writing, reading, and spelling. The overall theme is space and tests 2 and 3 have space related words. Test 1 is about spelling out numbers as words. Links to tests are on the menu bar and also on this page.

Test 1 shows numbers which must be spelled out in words. This is checking writing/spelling ability. It is for children around age 7.

Test 2 is a true or false quiz on if the spellings of different space related words are correct. It is for children aged around 9 years old.

Test 3 is about writing out the correct names for the images. It is for children around 8 years old. This is checking if the player knows the names for these images and also if they know how to spell them correctly.
